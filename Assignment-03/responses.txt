What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?:
    An IP Address is an address given to individual computers 
and devices to identify them on networks. A DNS translates
IP Addresses, as well as domains, into names that are 
easily memorized by people.

What is a cookie?:
    A cookie is information about a user that a website
archives for later use. This can include your veiwing 
history to target ads specifically aimed towards you. 

What are the three essential parts of a URL?:
    The protocol, the domain, and the path.
