What is Lane Street?

"Originally the name of my friends' minecraft server, I thought it was a fun oxymoron referencing my username. It's stuck ever since."

Lane Street is a Frankenstein site, featuring both school and passion projects created by @LaurenceLane, an artist and over-all pop-culture enthusiast. These projects consist of digital illustration, animation, comics, and the occasional hobby hyperfixation.


Who is Laurence?

Laurence is me! I use he/him and they/them. My work is primarily character design, but I tend to try a bit of everything, from wood-working to HTML/CSS. I tend to cut off sentences and thoughts strangely, my ADHD brain running too fast for my typing to keep up, so please be patient!
